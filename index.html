Welcome to Moon River K9!

At Moon River K9 we believe in creating an effective and balanced communication system for you and your dog through relationship and science based methods. If you are looking for just obedience or specialty training you have come to the right place! We offer a number of customizable training programs to help you and your furry companion succeed in real world situations. We will not only train your dog but will also teach you at the same time. All breeds, ages, and needs are welcome.
